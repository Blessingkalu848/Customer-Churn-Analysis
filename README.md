# Customer-Churn-Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview

This project presents an **interactive Customer Churn Analysis dashboard** built using Power BI to identify key drivers of customer attrition and support data-driven retention strategies.
The dashboard provides a comprehensive view of churn behavior across different customer segments, helping businesses understand **why customers leave and how to reduce churn**.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem
Customer churn is a critical challenge in subscription-based and telecom businesses. High churn rates lead to:
* Revenue loss
* Increased customer acquisition costs
* Reduced customer lifetime value
This project aims to analyze churn patterns and uncover actionable insights to improve customer retention.

---

## 🎯 Objectives
* Analyze overall churn rate and customer distribution
* Identify high-risk customer segments
* Understand the impact of:
  * Contract type
  * Tenure
  * Payment methods
  * Internet service
* Highlight customers with the highest churn risk and revenue impact

---

## 📊 Key Metrics (KPIs)

* **Total Customers:** 7,043
* **Churn Rate:** 26.54%
* **Average Monthly Charges:** $65
* **Average Tenure:** 32 months

## 📈 Dashboard Features

### 🔹 Risk Identification
* Top 10 high-risk customers by revenue

### 🔹 Interactive Filtering
* Slicers for:
  * Gender
  * Contract
  * Internet Service

---

## 🔍 Key Insights

* 📉 Customers on **month-to-month contracts** have the highest churn rate
* ⚠️ **Early tenure customers (0–1 year)** are most likely to churn
* 💳 **Electronic check users** show significantly higher churn
* 💰 Higher monthly charges are associated with increased churn risk
